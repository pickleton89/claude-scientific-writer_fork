## Executive Summary

This analysis examined productivity metrics, employee satisfaction, and operational costs across 847 employees over 18 months following our March 2024 remote work policy.

**Key Findings:**
1. **Productivity increased 23%**: Average task completion rose from 34 to 42 tasks/week
2. **Attrition dropped 31%**: Voluntary departures fell from 18% to 12.4% annually
3. **Collaboration declined 15%**: Cross-team project velocity decreased significantly
4. **Cost savings reached $1.2M**: Reduced facility and commuting subsidies

These results position the company to reinvest savings into growth initiatives while maintaining the flexibility that drives retention. However, declining collaboration threatens long-term innovation capacity.

**Recommendation:** Approve $400K for collaboration tool upgrades and quarterly in-person team events by Q2 2025. VP of Operations to present implementation plan by January 15.
