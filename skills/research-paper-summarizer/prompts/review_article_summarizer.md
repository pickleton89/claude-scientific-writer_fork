### 5. FRAMEWORKS & MODELS
- Does the review propose or refine any conceptual frameworks?
- Are classification systems or taxonomies presented?
- How useful are these organizing principles?

**Visual Models:** Note any schematic models that effectively capture complex relationships

---

### 6. KNOWLEDGE GAPS & CONTROVERSIES
- What does the review identify as unknown or unresolved?
- Where do experts disagree?
- What questions do the authors say need answering?

**Key Quote:** [Authors' statement on critical gaps]

---

### 7. CRITICAL ANALYSIS

**Strengths:**
- Is the coverage comprehensive and balanced?
- Does it provide genuine synthesis vs. just cataloging papers?
- Are the conclusions well-supported by cited evidence?

**Limitations & Biases:**
- What perspectives or literature might be underrepresented?
- Do the authors have potential conflicts of interest or theoretical biases?
- Is there cherry-picking of supportive studies?
- Are negative or null findings from the field discussed?
- Publication bias concerns in the underlying literature?

**Currency:** How up-to-date is the literature coverage? Note the most recent citations.

**Red Flags:** Note any overstatements, logical gaps, or unsupported assertions

---

### 8. TRANSLATIONAL & CLINICAL CONTEXT
- What is the current state of clinical translation in this area?
- What barriers exist between basic findings and patient benefit?
- Are regulatory or manufacturing challenges addressed?
- What clinical trials are ongoing or planned?

---

### 9. FUTURE DIRECTIONS
- What do the authors predict or recommend for future research?
- What emerging technologies might impact this field?
- What would constitute a breakthrough?

**Key Quote:** [Authors' vision for the field]

---

### 10. CREATIVE INSIGHTS & HIDDEN VALUE

Think beyond the explicit content:
- What papers in the reference list deserve closer reading?
- Are there connections to other fields not mentioned by the authors?
- What assumptions underlie the field that might be challenged?
- Are there "orphan" findings mentioned briefly that deserve more attention?
- What would a review written from a different perspective emphasize?
- Identify any emerging patterns across cited studies the authors didn't explicitly highlight

---

### 11. REFERENCE MINING
- List 5-10 primary research papers cited that seem most important to read
- Note any other reviews cited that might provide complementary perspectives
- Identify any methodological papers that describe key techniques
